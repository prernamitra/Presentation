I scream, You scream, We all scream for....ICE CREAM Why choose Scrumptious Scoop?

They are a well known name for the Ice Cream lovers. On a busy day they can sell over 1,000 scoops of ice-cream: such high turnover means their products are nearly always freshly made. Owner Prerna is trained as a chocolate maker and ice-cream maker in Belgium and Holland and all the store's ice-creams and sorbets are made in house. Owner Manika is a food artist and got her skills at the Food Institute France. Using the best ingredients - locally produced honeycomb pieces, Tahitian vanilla, Belgian chocolate and fresh fruit for the sorbets - the result is high-end excellence. Despite the wide range of flavours most customers go for Belgian Chocolate or Vanilla which are also recommended by the chefs.

You will not know Ice cream could taste like heaven till you walk into this parlour. You need to look at the reviews left by some authentic customers who swear by this place. Walk in for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
